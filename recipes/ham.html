Ham Recipe

Ingredient

  • 1 (5 pound) bone-in ham
  • 1 (8 ounce) can pineapple chunks in juice
  • ½ cup spicy brown mustard
  • 2 ½ tablespoons honey, or more to taste
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic

Steps

  1. Place ham in a slow cooker.
  2. Mix pineapple chunks, mustard, honey, and garlic in a bowl. Spread over ham.
  3. Cook on Low until ham is easily shredded with 2 forks, 6 to 8 hours.
