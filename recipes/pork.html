Pulled Pork

Ingredients

  • 1 (2 1/2 pound) boneless pork loin roast
  • water as needed
  • 1 (18 ounce) bottle barbeque sauce
  • 8 large sandwich buns, split

Steps

  1. Place pork in a slow cooker and add enough water to cover the bottom of the slow cooker.
  2. Cook on Low for 7 hours. Transfer pork to a bowl and shred with two forks. Return pork to slow cooker and stir in barbeque sauce. Continue to cook on Low for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
  3. Spoon a generous amount of pork into each roll to serve.
